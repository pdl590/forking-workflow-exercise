#Vegan-Lasagna

## Ingredients

- flour 
- water
- salt  
- tomato sauce
- vegetables (e.g. zucchini, eggplant, bell peppers)    
- vegan cheese (optional)


## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, water, and salt to make the pasta
    dough. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
3. Roll out the pasta dough on a floured surface until it is thin. Cut it into rectangular sheets.
4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of tomato sauce. Place a layer of pasta sheets on top of the sauce. Add a layer of sautéed vegetables and sprinkle with vegan cheese if desired. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce on top.
5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
6. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious vegan lasagna!  

## Tips

- For a quicker version, use store-bought pasta sheets instead of making your own.
- Pre-cook vegetables to reduce moisture and prevent a soggy lasagna.
- Make ahead: assemble the lasagna the day before and bake when ready.
- Nutritional info: serves 4-6 people, approximately 350-400 calories per serving.
