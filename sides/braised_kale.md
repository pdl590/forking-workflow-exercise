# Braised Kale with Bacon

Serve alongside [feijoada](../mains/traditional-feijoada.md), [farofa](../sides/farofa.md) and white rice.

## Ingredients
- **1 large bunch** kale (or collard greens), stems removed, finely chopped
- **100 g** bacon, chopped
- **2 cloves** garlic, thinly sliced or minced
- **1 tablespoon** olive oil (optional, if bacon is lean)
- **Salt and black pepper** to taste

## Instructions
1. **Render bacon:**
   - Place bacon in a cold skillet, cook over medium heat until browned and fat is rendered.

2. **Add garlic:**
   - Add garlic to the bacon and cook briefly, about 30-45 seconds, until fragrant. Do not let it burn.

3. **Cook kale:**
   - Add chopped kale in batches, tossing so it coats in the bacon fat.

4. **Braised finish:**
   - Cook for 3-6 minutes until tender but still bright green. Avoid overcooking.

5. **Season:**
   - Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

## Notes
- Slice the kale very thinly for the traditional texture.
- Keep it slightly firm; it should contrast the richness of the stew.
